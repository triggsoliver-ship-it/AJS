Who we are

Local, independent & on your side

AJS Vehicle Services is an independent garage based in Picket Piece, Andover, built on a simple idea — that drivers deserve top-quality work without paying main-dealer prices for things that add no real value.

We work on all makes and models. Whether it's a routine service, a stubborn warning light or a major repair, we use the latest diagnostic and workshop equipment to get the job done right — and we'll always explain what's needed and why, with no surprises on the bill.

Most of our work comes from personal recommendation and customers coming back time and again. That trust is something we never take for granted.
